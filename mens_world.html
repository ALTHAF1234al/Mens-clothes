Prestige The Man Store houses everything from exquisitely crafted suits, stylish casual wear, chic formal wear, elegant ethnic wear and accessories for men. Having been in the menswear sector since 1956, we have changed the outlook of menswear fashion in Bangalore. Our bespoke, tailored suits are particularly well known for their workmanship and fit. Our sales assistants will guide you through picking the fabric, the style and the fit best suited to your needs. Our fabric ranges from cotton, wool, silk and a whole lot more in patterns ranging from checks, stripes, paisley etcetera. Pick the fabric you desire and you will then be guided through the styles depending on the occasion you are looking to wear the suit. Our expert craftsmen will take your measurements and deliver the fit you deserve. We also have a range of tailored suits from various brands, including our very own, that are ready to be worn for those last minute events! A suit is a big and very important investment every man makes and it is crucial that you take the time to make the right decision. And we are here to help you and guide you through it all.
