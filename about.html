Our Story

We wanted to create an exclusive and premier shopping space for men.

Prestige the Man Store began in 1956 as Prestige House for Men on Commercial Street where it is now, a 600sq.ft store that now is 20,000 sq. ft of clothing, bespoke tailoring and accessories grew to extend over seven, precisely curated levels. Prestige Fashions Pvt. Ltd. is the umbrella organisation with all activity directed by Noaman Razack whose father Razack Sattar, a visionary and innovator, far ahead of his time, who founded the original establishment, Director.

Director's Message

“ At Prestige we’ve seen the city transform itself and may be we’ve helped raised its style quotient while learning so much ourselves. My father had a vision of Prestige and its role in Bangalore but I think even him, with his foresight, couldn’t have seen how far all this would go. Still he’s the one who took the first step and his ideals and convictions are even more meaningful today. So at this point in the journey, on the threshold of yet another major step, we at Prestige and over three generations of well-dressed Bangaloreans has him to thank”.

Noaman Razack-

Prestige The Man Store houses everything from exquisitely crafted suits, stylish casual wear, chic formal wear, elegant ethnic wear and accessories for men. Having been in the menswear sector since 1956, we have changed the outlook of menswear fashion in Bangalore. Our bespoke, tailored suits are particularly well known for their workmanship and fit. Our sales assistants will guide you through picking the fabric, the style and the fit best suited to your needs. Our fabric ranges from cotton, wool, silk and a whole lot more in patterns ranging from checks, stripes, paisley etcetera. Pick the fabric you desire and you will then be guided through the styles depending on the occasion you are looking to wear the suit. Our expert craftsmen will take your measurements and deliver the fit you deserve. We also have a range of tailored suits from various brands, including our very own, that are ready to be worn for those last minute events! A suit is a big and very important investment every man makes and it is crucial that you take the time to make the right decision. And we are here to help you and guide you through it all.
